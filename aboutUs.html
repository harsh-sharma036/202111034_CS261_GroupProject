Why E-Complaint Box ?



Need for E-complaint management system:

The purpose of e-Complaints is to bring more transparency into the system. An objective of settling complaints is that it allows organizations to
see where they need improvement and helps them to work on it.


Privacy Issues

There will be no fear of disclosure of your identity. Which further keeps you away from unnecessary issues.

A Formal Approach

The approach should be formal as it reaches the higher authorities of the organization. E-complaints provide us with a formal approach and give a better impression to resolve the stated issue.

Best option for Introverts

Many students hesitate a lot to complain about any issue. So, through e-complaints, they can easily make their voice heard.




  • In addition to these benefits, it also aids the organization to resolve issues in a handy way.

  • Also, the votes for a given complaint show its seriousness. So, it also helps the organization to decide whether action should be taken or not depending upon the seriousness of the issue.
